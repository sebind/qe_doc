Quantum ESPRESSO

Quantum ESPRESSO solves Kohn–Sham equations self-consistently from structured input files with NAMELISTS and INPUT_CARDS.

  • Required namelists: &CONTROL, &SYSTEM, &ELECTRONS
  • Optional namelists: &IONS, &CELL
  • Mandatory cards: ATOMIC_SPECIES, ATOMIC_POSITIONS, K_POINTS
